About this event

Join us for an afternoon of innovation and technology! The Computer Science Society is proud to present the Annual Spring Robotics Seminar. This year, we are focusing on AI integration in autonomous systems.

We will have guest speakers from leading tech companies, live demonstrations of student projects, and a networking session with alumni. Whether you are a seasoned coder or just curious about robotics, this event is for you.
